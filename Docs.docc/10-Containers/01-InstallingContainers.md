Step 1

Before packaging your Swift application into a portable container, set up the containerization tools on your
development machine. Think of this as preparing your workspace before starting a project - you need the right tools in
place first.

Your containerization journey starts with installation. Follow the comprehensive [Vessel documentation][1] to configure
everything properly. The Vessel team provides detailed guidance for the installation process and handles the specific
requirements for Apple's containerization ecosystem.

After installing these tools, you transform your Swift server application into a self-contained unit that runs
consistently across any environment - from your local machine to production servers in the cloud.

[1]: https://vessel.apple.com/documentation/vessel-desktop-doc/apple-containerization
